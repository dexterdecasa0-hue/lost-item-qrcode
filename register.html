Tips for Registration

  • Provide as much detail as possible to help others identify your item.
  • Use a recent photo if available (though not required here).
  • Check your email for updates after registration.
  • QR codes contain your contact info for easy sharing and scanning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I register?

Your item details are stored securely, and a QR code is generated containing your contact information. Others can scan it to get your details directly.

Is my information private?

Yes, the QR code displays your contact details only when scanned, and we encourage responsible sharing.

How long does it take to recover an item?

It varies, but many items are recovered within days. Check back regularly.

Registration Successful!

Your item has been registered. The QR code contains your contact info for finders.
